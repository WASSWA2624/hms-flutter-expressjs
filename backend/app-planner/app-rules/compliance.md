# Compliance Rules

Purpose: encode privacy, audit, and evidence obligations.

Rules:
- all mutations create audit evidence
- sensitive reads must be traceable when policy requires it
- PHI, payment, roster, and mortuary release actions require strong actor attribution
- retention and export behavior must be documented for regulated records
- break-glass usage, consent changes, PHI review, and release authorisations must be reviewable after the fact
- compliance logging must preserve who acted, what changed, where it happened, and under which scope
